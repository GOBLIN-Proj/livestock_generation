livestock_generation.livestock

This module is responsible for generating national livestock data, including the management of cohorts, animal data calculations, and scenario-based projections for agricultural systems. It utilizes various data sources and models to compute exports, productivity, and sustainability metrics for livestock production under different environmental and management scenarios.
